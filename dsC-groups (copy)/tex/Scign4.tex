\item  \scrText{Systems Pharmacology and Polypharmacology}: \txtItem{Polypharmacology 
			integrates physical models of chemical 
			and molecular structures with sociodemographic data on 
			drugs' effects.  This enables drug interactions 
			to be understood more rigorously and holistically.  
			For example, with proper data aggregation, initial 
			computational-chemical interaction models --- e.g., between 
			pharmaceuticals' molecular structures and target 
			proteins --- can evolve into a larger theory of 
			drugs' bioactive and chemo- physical effects in the context 
			of patients' social and biomedical situations.}
			
		\vspace{.5em}
			
			\item  \scrText{Medical Diagnostic Support and Image Analysis/Computational Geometry}:  
 \txtItem{Computational Discrete Geometry gives quantitative form to 
visual judgments which trained diagnosticians make when 
looking for diagnosticlly significant patterns and segments 
in medical images which.  By combining medicine, mathematics, 
and visualistics, this technology helps diagnoses to be made more quickly 
and improves data sharing between diagnosticians and care-givers.}
